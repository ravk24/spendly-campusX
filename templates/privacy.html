Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects the information you give us and a small amount that comes with using the service. From you: your account details — email address and password — and the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

Automatically: basic technical information such as your browser type and the pages you visit, which helps us keep the service running and diagnose problems. We do not ask for bank credentials, card numbers, or government identifiers, and you should not enter them into expense descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the service back to you: storing your expenses, building the summaries and category breakdowns you see, and letting you sign in securely.

We also use it to maintain and improve Spendly, and to contact you about your account when something important changes. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your expense records to target advertising at you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Spendly's database and kept for as long as your account is open. Passwords are stored hashed, never in plain text.

You can delete individual records or close your account at any time, which removes your data from the live service. Backups may retain copies for a limited period afterwards. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection — keep your own copies of anything you cannot afford to lose.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a few outside providers to operate — for example hosting, and web fonts loaded from Google Fonts when a page renders. These providers may receive limited technical information, such as your IP address, as a normal part of serving the site.

We share your data with third parties only where it is needed to run the service, or where the law requires it. Providers are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, or want to access or delete the data we hold about you, get in touch at privacy@spendly.example.

We may update this policy as Spendly changes. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Your use of Spendly is also governed by our Terms and Conditions.
